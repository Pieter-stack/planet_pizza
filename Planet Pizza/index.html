About Our Space Pizza Association

Planet pizza is an outer pizza shop with the attend of making the best pizza in the galaxy.
Planet pizza was founded by Pieter Venter in the early twenty first century. Our pizza sauce is made from ripe space tomatoes pick in late August and early September and fresh packed with fresh basil. Our pizza dough is made in zero gravity using fine mild flour from spring wheat. To finish our pizza we use 100% whole milk from the milkyway to make mozzarella.
Quick delivery and quality pizza has become a signature part of Planet pizza legacy. Our speciality is outer space delivery . Last minute orders are always welcome. Best pizza in the universe.
